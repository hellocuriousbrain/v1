Below Idioms are explained in lesson 2 :-<br />
<br />
See eye to eye = Do or say something to make things worse<br />
A gift of the gab = Talent for speaking/Ability to speak<br />
By Leaps and bound = Rapidly / Quickly / At rapid race .<br />
To foam at one’s mouth = To get very angry.<br />
By fits and starts = Irregularly / Occasionally <br />
bed of roses = Comfortable situation<br />
bed of thorns = Uncomfortable situation<br />
