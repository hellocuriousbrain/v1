Below Idioms are explained in lesson 1 :-<br />
<br />
Hen Pecked husband = A husband under the control of this wife<br />
Be born with a silver spoon = Be born in a rich family<br />
Throw dust into someone eyes = To mislead/ to deceive<br />
Rub salt into the sound = Do or say something to make things worse<br />
Put the cart before the horse = Do things in the wrong way<br />
A fish out of water = Uncomfortable<br />
Let cat out of the bag = exposed secret<br />
